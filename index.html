Why your POS choice matters more than you think

Most salon owners in Malaysia are still tracking packages on paper cards, calculating staff commission in Excel, and managing bookings via WhatsApp. That works — until it doesn't. A missed package deduction, a double booking, or a month-end commission dispute all cost real money and damage trust with your team and clients.

A purpose-built salon POS system solves these problems by automating the admin work so you and your staff can focus on delivering great service. But not all systems are built the same — and the wrong choice means paying for features you don't need, or missing ones you do.

This guide covers the top salon POS systems available in Malaysia as of 2026: what they do well, where they fall short, and which type of business each one suits best.

What to look for in a salon POS system

Before comparing products, here are the six things that actually matter for beauty businesses in Malaysia.

📦
Package & session tracking

Auto-deduct sessions on checkout. Manual counting leads to disputes and lost revenue.

📅
Appointment & booking

Online booking link + automated WhatsApp reminders to cut no-shows before they happen.

💰
Staff commission calculation

Auto-calculated per service rendered. Removes the monthly manual reconciliation nightmare.

🧾
LHDN e-Invoice compliance

From 2026, e-Invoicing is mandatory for all Malaysian taxpayers. Your system must support this.

📱
Mobile access

Most salon owners run their business from their phone. A system that requires a desktop is a dealbreaker.

💵
Transparent pricing

Watch for systems that hide fees, charge per-transaction, or require hardware purchases on top of subscription.
